Good questions

Quick answers.

Who owns the domain?

HelloSite registers and manages the domain on your behalf as part of the service. While you're an active customer, the domain is fully yours to use — we just handle the renewals and technical bits so you don't have to.

Can I change the content after launch?

Absolutely. Email any updates to cam@gethellosite.com — Starter plans get one small tweak per quarter, Executive includes one update per month, and larger edits are always quoted up front.

What if I already own a domain?

Mention it in the onboarding form. We'll either transfer it in (most common) or point it to your new site — whichever works better for your situation.

How do I cancel?

Email cam@gethellosite.com. No phone tree, no retention gauntlet. We'll stop billing at the end of the current cycle.
